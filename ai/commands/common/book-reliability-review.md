# Book Reliability Review

Use $book-reliability-review as the primary lens for this task.

Before editing or reviewing, briefly state:

- the production failure modes being considered
- the timeout, retry, resource, isolation, overload, and recovery risks
- the checks, tests, logs, or operational signals that will prove the result

Then proceed with the requested review or implementation unless essential context is missing. Keep the book lens subordinate to the user's instructions, repository instructions, and the project's existing architecture. Prefer bounded degradation and visible failure over hidden coupling.
